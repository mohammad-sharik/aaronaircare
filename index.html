Centrifugal Fan

An Industrial Centrifugal Fan is a high-performance air movement system designed to move air, gases, fumes, or dust in industrial environments. Unlike axial fans, centrifugal fans use a rotating impeller to increase air pressure and direct airflow at a 90-degree angle, making them ideal for high-pressure and heavy-duty applications.

INDUSTRIAL

Wet Scubber System

A Wet Scrubber System is an air pollution control device used to remove dust, fumes, gases, and harmful pollutants from industrial exhaust streams. It works by bringing contaminated air into contact with a scrubbing liquid (usually water or a chemical solution), which captures and neutralizes pollutants before clean air is released into the atmosphere.

Wind-Driven Turbo Ventilator

Energy-free natural ventilation system for industrial and commercial roofs. This wind-powered turbo ventilator continuously extracts hot air, fumes, smoke, and moisture from buildings without consuming electricity. Constructed with rust-proof SS304 stainless steel turbine and galvanized steel base. Self-lubricating sealed bearings ensure maintenance-free operation for years. Available in 12", 18", 24", and 36" throat sizes. Ideal for factories, warehouses, sheds, godowns, and industrial facilities. Each unit provides equivalent ventilation of 6-10 exhaust fans at zero operating cost. Easy rooftop installation with weatherproof flashing. Reduces heat buildup, improves air quality, and extends roof life by preventing condensation. Made in India for Indian climate conditions.

Manufacturing Facility – Rooftop Ductwork Installation

Complete insulated ductwork installation for a 1,00,000 sq.ft. manufacturing facility in Uttar Pradesh. Fabricated and installed 800+ linear meters of GI ductwork with 50mm glass wool insulation and aluminum cladding for rooftop routing. Project included supply and exhaust ducting networks, wind-driven turbo ventilators, structural supports with anti-vibration mounts, and weatherproof sealing. Our team coordinated duct layout with existing building infrastructure to minimize obstructions while ensuring optimal airflow distribution. System handles 2,00,000 CMH total air volume across production zones, warehouses, and office areas. All ductwork pressure-tested for leakages and certified leak-free before commissioning.
